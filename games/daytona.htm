Daytona USA is an arcade game by SEGA. It was originally released in arcades in March 1994. In addition to having a start of the art 3d workstation powering its graphics, it was one of the first arcade games to include a force feedback system. These impressive features made it very difficult to port accurately to the home environment. It was only properly ported on 2011/10/26, when it released on Xbox Live Arcade for the Microsoft Xbox 360. This was the first port with arcade authentic graphics. Via use of the Xbox 360 Wireless Force Feedback Steering Wheel, you can also enjoy the authentic driving experience. It's one of my favorite racing games, as someone who doesn't normally play them.
